How to Play Rummy

Objective

Be the last player standing by keeping your total score below the cutoff (100, 150, or 201 points).

Gameplay

  • ♦Each player is dealt 10 cards (7 if 4+ players)
  • ♥Draw from the pile or discard pile, then discard one card
  • ♠Form sets (3+ same rank) and runs (3+ consecutive same suit)
  • ♣Lay down all cards to "go out" and win the round

Scoring

  • ♦Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10 points
  • ♥Aces = 1 point (or 15 in some variations)
  • ♠Number cards = face value
  • ♣Round winner gets 0; others sum their hand

Strategy

  • ♦Hold low-value cards to minimize risk
  • ♥Watch what opponents pick from the discard pile
  • ♠Build both sets and runs for flexibility
  • ♣Don't wait too long - points add up fast
